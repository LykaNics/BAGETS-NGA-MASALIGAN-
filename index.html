BEGINNING A NEW GENERATION OF YOUTH IN AN EXCELLENT AND TRANSFORMATIVE SOCIETY

BARANGAY YOUTH ID

Pagkakakilanlan ng youth sa barangay, upang makapag-access sa mga serbisyo at benepisyo at pagkakaroon ng identida sa komudidad.

SUGOD KABATAAN SPORTS CLINIC

Programang nagbibigay ng mga serbisyo at pag-aalaga para sa mga atleta at mga taong interesado sa pagsasanay sa iba't ibang uri ng sports.

BARANGAY LEARNING RESOURCE CENTER

Ito ay institusyon sa barangay na may mahalagang papel sa pagpapalaganap ng edukasyon at kaalaman. Kalakip dito and Free Printing services, at ICT Center na ekslusibo sa kabataan ng Brgy. New Isabela.

KAHILWAYAN LGBT SOCIETY

Organisasyon na may kinalaman sa mga isyu at pangangailangan ng mga kabataang miyembro ng LGBT (Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender).

YOUNG HEROES EMERGENCY RESPONSE TEAM

Itatatag para sa layuning magbigay ng agarang tulong at pagtugon sa mga sakuna, kalamidan, o mga emergency situation sa Barangay New Isabela.

REVIVING 4H CLUB

Ang 4H Club ay isang samahang pang-edukasyon at pang-agrikultura para sa mga kabataan. Ito ay may temang "Head, Heart, Hands, and Health" na naglalayong magtaguyod ng kabataan na maging mga mamamayan na may mataas na moralidad, kaalaman sa agrikultura, at mabuting kalusugan.

TIMELY EDUCATIONAL ASSISTANCE & INCENTIVES

Programa na naglalayong suportahan ang edukasyon ng mga kabataan sa pamamagitan ng pagbibigay ng tulong pinansyal, scholarship, at mga insentibo sa mga academic achievers.

SOCIO-CULTURAL PROGRAMS AND ACTIVITIES

Ang layunin ng ganitong grupo ay palakasin ang kakayahan ng kabataan, magkaruon ng oportunidad na magpakita ng kanilang talento, at magkaruon ng positibong impluwensya sakanilang komunidad.

"BEA"

MA. BEATRIZ S. DORERO

Ma. Beatriz Santolaja Dorero was born on February 5, 2001, at Taguig City, Metro Manila, and before turning a year old, moved to Tacurong City, Sultan Kudarat, where she established residence, living with her mother Nancy Santolaja Dorero, and grandmother Asuncion Santolaja Dorero, together with her three dogs, Tris, Zeus and Burpy plus the two cats Night and Charly. She thrived academically during her elementary years at New Isabela Central Elementary School (NICES) and continued her education in Junior and Senior High School at Notre Dame-Siena College of Tacurong, Inc. Alongside her academic achievements, she excelled in her involvement in various activities both at the church and school, where she belongs to the Lectorate Ministry of Medalla Milagrosa Chapel. In 2021, her dedication to her community since she was child was recognized, when she was elected as the Sangguniang Kabataan Official of Barangay New Isabela, taking on the role of Budget Monitoring Officer and Chairman for the Committee on Health, Health Services, and Reproductive Health. She also paved the way for ZAVEL Band, the Official Youth Band of Barangay New Isabela, wherein in 2022, the band won as 1st Runner Up in the Linggo ng Kabataan 2022 Battle of the Bands City Wide Level. Recently, she finished college with the degree of Bachelor of Science in Nursing at Sultan Kudarat State University. Bea is an embodiment of a future healthcare professional and public servant leader. With a steadfast and empathetic commitment to helping others, her journey has been marked by an unwavering dedication and a burning desire to make a positive impact in the lives of those she encounters.
